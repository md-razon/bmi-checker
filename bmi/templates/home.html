Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI)



Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

BMI Calculating Procedure

Enter your weight and height using standard measures.
Then click Calculate BMI and see your BMI result.

Height conversion Procedure

Convert your height from feet-inches to inches.
Your Height in cm = (Your height in inches * 2.54)
